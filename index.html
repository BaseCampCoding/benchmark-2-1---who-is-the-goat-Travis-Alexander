WHO IS THE GOAT?

Tim Duncan

Tim Duncan is one of the best basketball players ever. He is, in fact, the greatest power foward of all-time. Duncan graduated from Wake Forest University leading the country in points, rebounds, and blocks. Check out more of his college stats here.

He then went on to be drafted #1 overall to the San Antonio Spurs. Here he helped lead them to 5 championships. A total of 4,374 players have appeared in at least one NBA game. Tim Duncan is one of the 26 players who have won 5 or more championships.

In his 19 seasons of playing, he won MVP twice (2001-02, 2002-03), ROTY(1997-98), made 15 All-Star apperances, won the NBA Finals 5 times, and won Finals MVP 3 times(1998-99, 2002-03, 2004-05). Check out more of his rewards here.
